Master Service Agreement (Sample)

This Master Service Agreement ("Agreement") is made effective as of March 15, 2025 by and between
Gamma Solutions Ltd., 45 Industrial Road, London, UK ("Client"), and DeltaWorks Pvt. Ltd., 101
Innovation Drive, Bangalore, India ("Contractor").

1. Services: Contractor shall provide software development, maintenance, and support services as set
forth in individual Task Orders.

2. Term: The Agreement shall commence on the Effective Date and remain in force for an initial term of
one (1) year, thereafter automatically renewing for successive one (1) year terms unless either party
provides written notice of non-renewal at least thirty (30) days prior to the end of the then-current term.

3. Payment Terms: Client shall pay Contractor monthly within thirty (30) days of receipt of invoice. All
amounts are in GBP and exclusive of applicable taxes. Late payments attract interest at 1.5% per
month.

4. Limitation of Liability: Except for liability arising from gross negligence, willful misconduct, or breach
of confidentiality, each party's aggregate liability under this Agreement shall not exceed the total fees
paid by Client to Contractor in the twelve (12) months preceding the claim.

5. Indemnity: Contractor shall indemnify Client against third-party claims arising from Contractor's
negligent acts, provided that Contractor's indemnity shall be limited to direct damages only and shall
not cover punitive or consequential damages.

6. Termination: Either party may terminate for convenience upon sixty (60) days' written notice. For
material breach, the non-breaching party may terminate if the breach remains uncured thirty (30) days
after written notice.

7. Confidentiality: Each party agrees to protect Confidential Information as set forth in the
Confidentiality Exhibit attached hereto.

8. Governing Law: This Agreement shall be governed by the laws of England and Wales.
